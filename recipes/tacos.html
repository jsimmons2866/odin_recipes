Taco Recipe

This is a quick, family recipe on how to make tacos.

Ingredients list

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • Some hard shell tacos

Now it's time to make it. Here's how:

  1. Brown the meat in a pan on the stove.
  2. Drain excess grease from meat.
  3. Add taco seasoning to meat and 3/4 cup water..
  4. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Warm shell(s) in microwave for 8 seconds.
  6. Fill shell with some meat, add cheese to preference.
  7. Eat.
