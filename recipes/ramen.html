Ramen Recipe

This is a quick, family recipe on how to make Ramen!

Ingredients list

  • Pot
  • Some water
  • Stove
  • Ramen pack

Now it's time to make it. Here's how:

  1. Turn stove on.
  2. Fill pot with water.
  3. Boil water.
  4. Once boiling, add ramen noodles to water. DO NOT ADD THE SEASONING YET!
  5. Boil for 3 minutes.
  6. Once noodles have cooked for 3 minutes, empty seasoning packet into ramen.
  7. Cook for 1 more minute with seasoning.
  8. Empty pot into bowl and turn off stove.
