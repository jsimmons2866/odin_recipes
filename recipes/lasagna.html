Lasagna Recipe

This is a quick, family recipe on how to make lasagna - the tasty way.

Ingredients list

  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pount lasagna noodles, oven ready
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 32oz spaghetti sauce
  • 16oz pizza sauce
  • 1 egg

Now it's time to make it. Here's how:

  1. Combine ricotta cheese, milk, and egg together in a bowl.
  2. Line bottom of pan with noodles.
  3. Cover noodles with spaghetti sauce, then cheese mixture.
  4. Repeat this three times.
  5. Cover top lay in mozz cheese.
  6. Bake uncovered at 350 for 45 minutes.
  7. Take off cover and bake for 15 more minutes.
